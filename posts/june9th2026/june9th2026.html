Abstract (a summary in plain language)

This blog post is formatted similar to a typical research paper. The feeling of looking at a dense scientific paper for the first time is confusing and overwhelming, filled with buzz words and academic terminology crafted to sound as smart as possible. I imagine that same feeling is the same as whenever I summarize what I do for work. In this post I’m going to both explain how to read and digest a research paper AND what I’ll be doing this summer for my internship. Hopefully by the end you’ll have a better grasp on both.
